Applications of GIS & Remote Sensing in Water Leakage Management

By integrating GIS and Remote Sensing technologies, BUWASA enhances its ability to monitor, predict, and resolve water leakage more efficiently. These tools allow us to:

  • Identify high-risk leak zones
  • Map water infrastructure and coverage
  • Track environmental impacts using remote data
  • Improve communication between field technicians and control centers

This integrated approach leads to better service delivery, and improved customer satisfaction.
